DEDICATION

For those who searched, questioned, and found truth

To the silent, the overlooked, the questioned, and all who dared to ask “why.”

To every soul who sat in the sanctuary but still felt unseen.

To those who were handed tradition but hungered for truth.

To the ones who were raised in the presence of power but still left with pain.

And to Bethesda Tabernacle,
Thank you for being the place that stirred my questions, pressed me deeper, and unknowingly led me into a hunger for more than I had been taught.

It was in that silence, the longing, and even the wrestle that I found Him; not as part of a divided Godhead, but as the One True God whose name is Jesus.

My hope is that every line in these pages touch the wounds that tradition could not soothe.

May your heart draw strength, peace, and boldness from The One True Name that brings salvation, the Name of Jesus.

May you discover what I found for myself; Jesus was never part of the answer but is the whole answer, the TRUTH.

“And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free.” John 8:32
