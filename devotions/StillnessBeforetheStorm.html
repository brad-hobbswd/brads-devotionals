Stillness Before the Storm

Psalm 46:10‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬‬

Devotion:

Some mornings feel like a gift, the air is still, the coffee’s just right, and the world hasn’t made any demands yet. In these quiet moments, it’s easier to hear the whisper of God reminding you, “I’m here. I’m with you.”

Stillness isn’t just about silence, it’s about awareness. It’s a sacred pause that lets your soul breathe. Before the day begins to pull at you, God wants to anchor you.

This verse reminds us that discipleship isn’t just about sitting back and listening. It’s about having a relationship with the Word of God. That means learning it, thinking about it, obeying it, and letting it shape every part of our lives.

So don’t rush past this peace. Let it shape your attitude, your responses, and your outlook. Let the calm become your compass; a reminder that you don’t carry this day alone.

Whether the rest of this day holds joy or stress, you’ve already met with the One who holds it all together.

Prayer:

Lord, thank You for this moment of stillness. Help me carry this peace into everything I do today. Whatever comes, remind me that You remain in control and that my day has already begun in Your presence. In Jesus name, amen.
