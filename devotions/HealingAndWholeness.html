Healing & Wholeness

Restoration begins where Jesus is welcomed in

Healing often happens quietly, beneath the surface, where God works within places others cannot see. Wounds may remain hidden, and brokenness may go unspoken, yet nothing is unseen by Jesus.

Wholeness is not the absence of pain, though the presence of restoration. God moves toward what has been damaged, bringing healing into places that once felt unreachable. What was broken can be restored. What was lost can be rebuilt.

This process requires patience, honesty, and surrender. Healing does not rush or perform. It unfolds as trust deepens and truth replaces what once caused harm.

These devotions guide you through that journey, helping you release what has been carried too long and receive what God freely gives.
